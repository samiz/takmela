Takmela: Siblings Datalog Example

Another classic example to showcase Logic Programming.

Program 
parent("a", "b").
parent("b", "c").
parent("a", "d").
parent("d", "e").

    parent(P, X),
    parent(P, Y),
    X != Y
    .
